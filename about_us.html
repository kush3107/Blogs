SOCIETY FOR EDUCATIONAL EXCELLENCE

Society for Educational Excellence (SEE), under the chairmanship of Shri Ved Prakash Goel, was established in 1999 with a dream to provide composite and quality training to young minds in Engineering & Management Education so that they become efficient technocrats & promising managers, ready to face tough challenges in the competitive world and also prove to be good human beings to serve the society at large. To make this dream come true SEE established “Academy of Business and Engineering Sciences (ABES)” in 2000 offering B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering, Information Technology and Electronics and Communication Engineering along with Master in Computer Applications, with due approval from AICTE, New Delhi and afflition to UP Technical University, Lucknow having UPTU code 032. In the following years Mechanical Engineering (2003), Master in Business Administration (2006), Electrical and Electronics Engineering (2008), Civil Engineering (2011) and M.Tech. (CSE, ECE, & ME) were also introduced. In 2009, Academy of Business and Engineering Sciences (ABES) was renamed as ABES Engineering College with UPTU Code 032 after due approval, having B.Tech., MBA and MCA programs. Along with this another Institution, Academy of Business & Engineering Sciences with UPTU Code 459 with MCA program was also retained.
